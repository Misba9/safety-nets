FAQ

What are safety nets?
Safety nets are protective systems or tools designed to safeguard individuals or groups from injury or harm. They can refer to physical barriers used in construction and sports or to social systems that provide financial or social assistance.
What types of safety nets are available?
We offer a variety of safety nets, including:
• Construction Safety Nets: Used to prevent falls on building sites.
• Sports Nets: Designed for sports venues to ensure safety.
• Bird Protection Nets: Used to prevent birds from entering certain areas.
• Safety Nets: Installed in homes or balconies to protect children.
Why should I install a safety net?
Safety nets protect people, property, and assets from accidents and injuries. They reduce the risk of falls, prevent accidents in high-risk areas, and offer peace of mind for families and organizations.
Are bird protection nets harmful to birds?
No, our bird nets are designed to safely prevent birds from entering certain areas without causing them harm. They are environmentally friendly and non-intrusive.
Can safety nets be installed in balconies or windows?
Yes, child safety nets are commonly installed on balconies, windows, and staircases to prevent falls and ensure child safety in homes.
Are child safety nets easy to remove when not needed?
Yes, child safety nets can be easily removed when no longer required, and they do not cause damage to walls or railings.
